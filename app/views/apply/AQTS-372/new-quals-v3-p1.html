Teaching qualification guidance

Please review the below guidance before entering your teaching qualifications on the next page.

Qualification criteria for India

Your teaching qualification must be from an institution that is recognised by the National Council for Teacher Education.

We cannot accept a Diploma in Education, Diploma in Teacher Education or a Trainer Teachers' Certificate from India as these do not meet the required level.

Age and subject restrictions

We only accept applications from teachers from [Country] who are qualified to teach children aged 11-16 in one or more of the following specific subjects:

Maths, Science, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Latin, Mandarin, Russian, Spanish

Qualified means:

  • Your teaching qualification specifically states that you can teach one of the subjects listed above, for example, the subject is in the title of your teaching qualification
  • Your teaching qualification certificate shows at least 25% of your teaching qualification was in one of the listed subjects
  • Your bachelor’s degree certificate shows at least 50% of your degree was in one of the listed subjects

We accept any of the above as evidence you are qualified to teach a specific subject.

More than one teaching qualification

If you have more than one teaching qualification enter the first one you received that qualifies you to teach. For example, if you have a bachelor's and master's degree, and both qualify you to teach, enter the bachelor's degree here.

You can add more degrees later.
