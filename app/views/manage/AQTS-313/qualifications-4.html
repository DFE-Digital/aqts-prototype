Exempt from English language

Check:

  • applicant holds qualifications that meet the required academic level (level 6 academic qualification or equivalent)
  • teaching qualifications were completed in an eligible country (for example, we cannot award QTS where they applied through Spain, but their teaching was completed in South Africa)
  • they’re qualified to teach in mainstream education (not vocationally only/SEN only/early years/pre-school only)
  • the applicant is qualified to teach children aged 11-16
  • their teaching qualification specifically qualifies them to teach either Maths, Science, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Latin, Mandarin, Russian or Spanish (OR they have a teaching qualification for children aged 11-16 AND at least 50% of their degree was in one of those subjects)
  • teacher qualification certificate is present (and translation if required)
  • teacher qualification transcript is present (and translation if required)
  • university degree qualification certificate is present (and translation if required)
  • university degree transcript is present (and translation if required)
  • additional degree qualification certificate is present (and translation if required)
  • additional degree transcript is present (and translation if required)
